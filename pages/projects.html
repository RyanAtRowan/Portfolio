Hook or be Hooked

A multiplayer game where you can play as either a fish or a hook. Join as a player or spectator and see who comes out on top!

Features:

  • Player Roles: Play as a Fish, dodging the hook, or as the Hook, trying to catch the fish. If the player roles are already taken, you'll be assigned the Spectator role.
  • Spectator Mode: As a spectator, you can throw trash into the game by tapping or clicking the screen to make things more difficult for the hook.
  • Real-time Multiplayer: All players and spectators are connected through SignalR, which ensures all actions are updated in real-time.

Red Book

A Web App that will allow you to select your favorite Maplestory Scrolls and assign prices to them.

Features:

  • User Login: users can log in and maintain their own favorite scroll list
  • Scroll Search: search through hundreds of scrolls, you may use drop down menus to select which particular attribute you would like to search for.
  • Price Adjust: users can adjust the prices of the items and update the current value of the items in the data base.
  • Top 10: queries the database and grabs the top 10 highest price scrolls in the game.
